## 🌟 About NebulaFinance

**NebulaFinance** is an **AI-powered personal finance platform** designed to help users **track, understand, and optimize their money** *passively and ethically*.

Unlike traditional finance apps that only display numbers, NebulaFinance **analyzes spending patterns**, **predicts financial trends**, and **offers intelligent suggestions** without forcing user actions.

> 🚫 No buying/selling  
> 🔍 No dark patterns  
> 🤖 Pure insights & clarity  

---

## 🧠 Highlighted Technology — RAG-Based Financial Intelligence

Instead of intrusive AI or rule-based alerts, NebulaFinance runs a **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) AI system** silently in the background.

### 🔍 How It Works

- Daily transactions are embedded and stored securely
- Financial context is retrieved dynamically
- AI generates **context-aware insights**, not generic tips
- System continuously recalculates:
  - Daily average spend
  - Monthly savings trend
  - Category-wise anomalies
- Insights adapt as user behavior evolves

### 🎯 Result

- Passive money management
- Zero manual effort from users
- Ethical, explainable AI decisions
- Clear suggestions instead of commands

## 💼 Business Model (Detailed)

NebulaFinance follows a **freemium + insight-driven model**, optimized for trust and long-term user value.

### 🆓 Free Tier
- Expense & income tracking
- Daily and monthly averages
- Category insights
- Basic AI suggestions
- Visual dashboards

### 💎 Premium Tier (Future Scope)
- Advanced RAG-based forecasting
- Long-term savings projections
- Personalized financial goals
- AI council-powered research insights
- Exportable financial reports

### 🏢 Enterprise / B2B (Future)
- White-label finance dashboards
- Employee finance wellness tools
- Aggregated (privacy-safe) analytics
- Subscription-based deployment

> 💡 Revenue is **not** driven by selling user data — privacy is core.
